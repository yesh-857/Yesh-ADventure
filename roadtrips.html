Road Trips & Bike Routes

Sample plans you can tweak. Replace city names with your own starting point.

Ladakh Bike Circuit (10–12 Days)

  • Route: Manali → Jispa → Sarchu → Leh → Nubra → Pangong → Leh → Manali
  • Distance: ~1,600–1,800 km
  • Difficulty: Hard (altitude, weather)
  • Highlight: Khardung La, Pangong Lake, insanely beautiful roads.

Weekend Ghat Ride (2–3 Days)

  • Route: City → Hill Station (Ghats) → Nearby Viewpoints → City
  • Distance: 400–700 km total
  • Difficulty: Medium
  • Highlight: Hairpin bends, chill weather, chai stops with views.

Coastal Escape (3–4 Days)

  • Route: City → Coastal Town 1 → Coastal Town 2 → City
  • Distance: 600–900 km
  • Difficulty: Easy–Medium
  • Highlight: Beach sunsets, seafood, empty early-morning highways.

First-Timer Dawn Ride (1 Day)

  • Route: City → 60–80 km viewpoint / breakfast spot → City
  • Distance: 120–160 km
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Highlight: Learn group riding, cornering, and discipline safely.
