Adventure Destinations

Some of my favourite (and dream) places for trekking, riding, and exploring. You can modify the details as you explore more.

Ladakh

Best time: June – September

Vibe: High-altitude passes, cold deserts, blue lakes.

Good for: Multi-day bike trips, photography, star-gazing.

Spiti Valley

Best time: June – October

Vibe: Raw, remote, dramatic landscapes & monasteries.

Good for: Experienced riders & mountain lovers.

Western Ghats

Best time: October – February

Vibe: Green hills, waterfalls, misty hairpin bends.

Good for: Weekend rides, short treks, monsoon drives.

Coastal Highways (Konkan / ECR)

Best time: November – February

Vibe: Sea on one side, villages on the other, smooth tarmac.

Good for: Relaxed long drives & sunrise rides.

Local Hills Near You

Best time: Year-round (avoid heavy rain)

Vibe: Quick escape from city, perfect for early morning rides.

Good for: Building confidence before big rides.
